Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Union is operated by Solomon Kelechi Enyinnaya

1. We don't record you

Union uses WebRTC peer-to-peer. Your video, audio, and screen share go directly between you and other participants. We do not store, record, or view your calls.

2. What we do collect

We only collect anonymous data to keep the service running: room IDs, timestamps, and basic error logs. No accounts. No personal data stored.
